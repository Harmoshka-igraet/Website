“Freshers Party”.txt

During 2024, I was a part of TOKYO board as Artistic activity and Outreach representative. One of my responsibilities was to design and organize visual and artistic events.

In October 2024, had organized the event for a first year students in ARTs. The event was called “Freshers Party” and was supposed to be a peaceful and relaxing event. To showcase that, I had included the snail imagery, which was meant to help students “slow down” and include TOKYO iconic spiral within the design.

Storyboard for Application usage .txt

During April 2024, I was participating in a Thematic Studio II course, where my team was creating a sleep and period tracking application.

One of the prerequisites of the course was the creation of a user usage storyboard. I was in charge of visuals of that specific part of the course.

My main goals were:

  • Create non-gender specific user visual
  • Showcase the uniqueness of the application
  • Shine light on the pain points of our potential user group within the storyboard
